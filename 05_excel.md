## Excel basic commands

|Shortcut  | operations  |
|----------|-------------|
|Ctrl+E    | Flash fill |
|Ctrl+D    | down fill |
|Ctrl+R    | right fill |
|Ctrl+1    | formatting cell |
|Ctrl+space| select column of the active cell |
|Ctrl+shift+space| select whole data |
|Ctrl+shift++| create a new row/column|
| Ctrl + ` | shows the forlmula underneath|
